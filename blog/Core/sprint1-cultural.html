Personal Core Values

"Core values are the fundamental beliefs of a person or organization. These guiding principles dictate behavior and can help people understand the difference between right and wrong."


Based on the above definition I can assert that my personal core values are Honesty, Kindness and Respect. I am an honest person―the truth always matters to me more than anything else. If I value this quality so much is because when I was in Middle School I was the one who did not behave correctly: “Let him who is without sin cast the first stone”, right? Here’s what happened: I cheated during a Music test. I hadn't studied as well as I should have so I kept my book opened with all my notes under my desk and I shamelessly copied all the answers. Well, obviously I got caught by my teacher. I was not so smart as I thought, after all. He took both my test and my book without any admonition, I felt so ashamed of myself. And so what? You would say. The following day ,during an after school activity - choir practice - my Music teacher had the brilliant idea to return the book to me and tell everybody what I did. It was so humiliating and for a person like me, shy and insecure, it was a pretty bad beating.
