My Learning Plan

Foundations Learning Plan

My long term goal and/or career pathway

I aim to become a front-end developer and specialize in Web Design. I think DevAcademy is a good start-point. After graduation, I hope to find a job in my field of interest and continue to develop my skills and increase my knowledge.

Non-technical - human skills I would you like to see developed in myself while at EDA

I am an introvert by nature, so I like spending time on my own - for example, during my lunch break at work, I have always looked for an empty table. EDA could be a good opportunity to improve my social skills. Another aspect of my personality I would like to work on, is my fear of public speaking that I probably have since I was born. By doing projects’ presentation I hope to overcome this state of anxiety that overwhelms me every time.

Description of your own strengths and limitations

My key-strengths are:
Supportiveness : despite my introvert nature, I more than happy to help coworkers getting projects finished on time or listen to friends’ concerns and find ways to resolve them. I take pleasure in finding satisfying resolutions to day-to-day challenges.
Determination & Consistency : I always do my best to complete a task or reach my goals, especially when I think they are right and all the efforts are worthwhile.
Transparency & Accountability : I am not able to hide my emotions or my doubts even if I wanted to, they would be written all over my face. This helps me to begin a genuine relationships and let others see me for who I really am, they can trust me and this increases accountability.
My weaknesses are:
Introversion : even if I enjoy my own company, I can see the limitations that this aspect of my character can bring. Sometimes I find it difficult to be around others and it takes time for me to build a relationship with my colleagues or the thought of speaking at a meeting send me into a cold sweat.
Asking for help as a last resort : usually I try to solve a problem by myself, from one hand because I am a bit proud and I don’t want to be judged , and from the other hand because I see a challenge as a way to put myself to the test.
Taking criticism personally: I don’t consider myself a touchy person and the reason why sometimes I don’t like criticism is because I see them like a failure and I get angry at myself. The only judgement I don’t accept is the non-constructive one, which often leads to hurt feelings and demotivation.

How I will manage my workload in this programme & how I will manage myself to work productively and safely with other people

I will use a time management tool, like Toggl or Pomodoro, to monitor and set the maximum time I can spend looking for a solution. If I am not able to solve a problem within that time frame I will ask someone for help and I will share my knowledge in order to find a quick solution together: two heads are better than one!
I will try to engage myself in social activities offered by EDA, like for example yoga classes, and spend time with my peers (taking a cup of coffee together during a break or having lunch together) to know each other better.
I will accept and respect opinions or criticism from other learners, facilitators and community representatives, by keeping in mind that they are here to help me not judge me.

How and where I will seek help in a timely way

As mentioned before, I would try to solve the problem by myself, breaking it down into smaller pieces and then probably by looking for some help online, doing some research on Google. If I could not find a solution in a reasonable amount of time, I would ask for help from my facilitator or colearners, depending on the type of problem I am working on.

What I expect from the facilitation team

I expect to receive help and guidance when requested during both the foundation and bootcamp. I would like to receive constructive feedback about my work and engagement.

Scheduling information

When I left my job to start my journey as a software developer, I decided to invest my time and attention completely to Enspiral DevAcademy. I'm still trying to cut out some time for my hobbies and for myself though, as I think well-being is an important element for a balanced life.
